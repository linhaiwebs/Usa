Frequently Asked Questions

What does the Health Score measure?

The Health Score combines multiple technical indicators -- RSI, MACD, moving averages, and volume trends -- into a single 0-100 rating that reflects current technical conditions. It is not a buy/sell recommendation.

Is this tool free to use?

Yes, basic stock diagnosis is completely free. Advanced features including detailed PDF reports and historical comparisons are available with registration.

How current is the data?

Market data is 15-minute delayed per exchange regulations. Real-time data may be available through broker API integration. This tool is for educational purposes only.
