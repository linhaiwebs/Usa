Understanding Indicators

How to use RSI? expand_more

RSI above 70 may suggest overbought conditions; below 30 may suggest oversold conditions. These thresholds are commonly used for educational context, not trading signals.

What is a MACD Golden Cross? expand_more

A Golden Cross occurs when the MACD line crosses above the signal line, which some analysts interpret as a potential trend shift.

Are institutional flows important? expand_more

Institutional flows can reflect large-scale positioning changes, providing context about how major market participants are allocating capital.
